Exploring British Columbia's Parks

My Outdoor Addiction

British Columbia is home to numerous beautiful parks. Some are easy and accessible, while others can challenge even the bravest adventurers. There are many activities to do in BC parks: hiking, camping, photography, skiing, snowshoeing, fishing, and mountian biking are just a few of the draws that bring millions of visitors a year.

My favorite activity is photographing the many beautiful landscapes and small creatures of the Pacific North-West. You can be sure, if a Mountian Lion shows up, I am out of there!
